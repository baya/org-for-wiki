** 分页查询
- [kaminari](https://github.com/amatsuda/kaminari)
- 用法
rails g kaminari:config  
@users = User.order(:name).page params[:page]
<%= paginate @users %>
- 实例
控制器
I18n.locale = :zh
@contests = Contest.order('created_at DESC').page(params[:page]).per(20)
